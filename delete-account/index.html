Delete Your W2W Account

You can delete your W2W account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete from the W2W Mobile App (Recommended)

  1. Open the W2W app on your mobile device
  2. Log in to your account (you must be logged in to delete)
  3. Tap on the Profile tab in the bottom navigation
  4. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right corner
  5. Scroll down and tap "Account"
  6. Tap "Delete Account"
  7. Read the warning message: "All associated data will be permanently deleted. This cannot be undone."
  8. Type "CONFIRM" in the text field to confirm deletion
  9. Tap "Delete" button
  10. You will be logged out automatically
  11. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app or cannot complete the in-app deletion process, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to: fnmghaithi@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "W2W Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your username (if you remember it)
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your W2W account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Name, username, email address, date of birth, gender, profile picture
  • Wardrobe Data: All uploaded clothing items and their photos
  • Outfits: All created outfits and outfit combinations
  • Photos: All images you uploaded, including selfies and clothing photos
  • Social Media Links: Any linked social media profiles
  • AI Data: AI credits balance, usage history
  • Preferences: All saved settings and preferences
  • Activity History: Your app usage data and interactions

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for a limited time for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes:

  • Transaction Records: Purchase history and subscription records (required for tax and accounting compliance) - retained for up to 7 years
  • Security Logs: Anonymized security and fraud prevention data - retained for up to 90 days
  • Legal Compliance: Data required to be retained by law - retention period varies by jurisdiction

All retained data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you personally.

Subscription Cancellation

Note: Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your subscription. You must cancel your subscription separately to avoid future charges.

To Cancel Your Subscription:

For iOS (App Store):

  1. Open Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find W2W in the list
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

For Android (Google Play):

  1. Open Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Tap "Payments & subscriptions" → "Subscriptions"
  4. Find W2W in the list
  5. Tap "Cancel subscription"

Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate (data deleted within seconds)
  • Email Request: Processed within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete (for email requests only)
